About Us

                   Welcome to CarsPoint - India's largest auto media vehicle. CarsPoint has always striven to serve car buyers and owners in the most
comprehensive and convenient way possible.We provide a platform where car buyers and owners can research, buy, sell and come together to discuss and talk about their cars.

Our Mission:

                   Our mission is to bring joy and delight into car buying and ownership.
To achieve this goal, we aim to empower Indian consumers to make informed car buying and ownership decisions with exhaustive and un-biased 
information on cars through our expert reviews, owner reviews, detailed specifications and comparisons. We understand that a car is by and
large the second-most expensive asset a consumer associates his lifestyle with.

A Brief History:

                   CarsPoint was launched in July 2015 by Harinarayanan.S. Since then we have been credited with several initiatives for Indian car consumers.
In 2016, we became the first website in India to consolidate Super cars inventory across dealers and present it to car buyers as an online marketplace.

Awards:

                  Along the way, we have been recognised for a few achievements.
Red Herring Global 200 (2015) – We were recognised as one of the world’s top 200 innovative IT companies by Red Herring.
Red Herring Asia 100 (2015 and 2016) – We were recognised as one of Asia’s top 100 innovative IT companies in both 2015 and 2016.

Group:

                  In Jan 2016, CarsPoint and CarTrade merged to become market leaders in both the new cars and Super cars space.
